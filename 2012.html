Bees are small insects that play a very important role in nature and the environment. They are responsible for pollination, a process that helps plants grow, reproduce, and produce fruits and seeds. Many foods people eat every day, such as fruits, vegetables, and nuts, depend on bees for pollination. Bees also produce honey and support healthy ecosystems by helping plants thrive. Protecting bees is essential for maintaining biodiversity and food production around the world.

Why Bees Are Important

Without bees, many plants and fruits would not grow. Bees help keep our environment healthy.
